 Italian Culture: Contributions to America 








From Frank Sinatra and Liza Minnelli to Madonna and Bon Jovi, Italian Americans have been making significant contributions to American popular music for nearly a century. Since Italian is the language of love, it only makes sense that Italians would bring us some of the greatest romantic melodies and love songs.
