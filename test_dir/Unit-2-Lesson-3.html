Origins of African-American Culture






   

As you learned in an earlier installment of Arts Alive, tap dancing is a distinctly American art form. It was created through a mixture of traditional African dance rhythms and Irish and British clogging steps. This created a style called “buck and wing,” which became modern tap dancing.

Fun Fact
  • The taps on a tap shoe's heels are like a bass drum. They create a lower sound. The taps on the toes produce a higher sound. So, tap dancing is like drumming.
