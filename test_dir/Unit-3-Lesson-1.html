Origins of Irish-American Culture








One of the most important instruments in traditional Irish music is the fiddle – also known as the violin. Irish fiddle music is popular all around the world, particularly influencing the styles on trained violinists. Bob Banerjee has been performing in the Irish fiddle tradition for many years. He is a former member of the internationally acclaimed Celtic group Gaelic Storm and performs regularly with the Pittsburgh-based Corn Beef and Curry Band. A native of Calcutta, India, Banerjee recently shared his thoughts on the influence of the Irish fiddle.
