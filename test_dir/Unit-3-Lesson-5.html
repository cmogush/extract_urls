Origins of Irish-American Culture






The Irish are known for their wit, wisdom, and knowledge of life gained through both great suffering and tremendous joy. David Whalen is a proud Irish-American Pittsburgh-based actor and professor of drama. In this segment of Arts Alive! he shares his thoughts on how Irish culture has shaped American culture, particularly through the art of drama. Whalen also shares his performance of a short but wisdom-filled monologue from John Patrick Shanley’s Pulitzer-Prize winning drama Doubt.
